Purpose of the System

The Online Leave Management System is designed to simplify the process of applying for, reviewing, and approving leave requests. It is primarily intended for use by university staff and students to streamline the administrative effort required for managing leave.

Key Features:

  • Online leave application form with key personal and academic details.
  • System records the leave type, duration, and reason.
  • Administrative approval and record keeping made simple.
  • Reduces paperwork and increases accountability.

How the System Works

  1. User navigates to the Apply page.
  2. Fills in required personal and academic details.
  3. Chooses the type of leave and provides a reason.
  4. Application is submitted and reviewed by the admin.
  5. Approval or rejection notification is provided to the applicant.
